Smart Features for Smart Organization

Instant Generation

Create your personal Memory Tape within seconds. Just upload the photos captured at a place, fill in the date, location, and a short background story—and MemoTape automatically transforms them into a beautifully structured .memo file. No hassle, no complexity—just your memories, neatly organized.

File Encryption

Every memory you create stays truly yours. MemoTape applies built-in file encryption to ensure your .memo files remain private and protected. Whether you store them locally or share them, your data stays encrypted end-to-end—accessible only through the MemoTape Reader.

Quick Share

Memories become even more special when shared. With MemoTape, you can easily send your .memo files to friends, family, or anyone you want to relive those moments with. When opened through the reader, the story-like flow brings your moments to life—exactly the way you experienced them.

Our Mission:

MemoTape was created from a simple idea:

What if memories could be preserved as vivid, story-like experiences? With MemoTape, users can generate a .memo file by uploading photos from a specific moment—complete with the date, location, and a short background story. Each .memo file captures the essence of that memory. And when viewed through the MemoTape Reader, those moments come alive again in a flowing, narrative experience. Your memories, told like a story.
